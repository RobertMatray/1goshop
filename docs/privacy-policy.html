1GoShop - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Overview

1GoShop ("the App") is a shopping list application developed by Robert Matray. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles your data.

Local Data Storage

All shopping list data is primarily stored locally on your device using standard device storage. The App does not require user accounts or registration.

Shared Lists (Firebase)

If you use the list sharing feature, your shopping list data is transmitted to Firebase Realtime Database (servers operated by Google) to enable real-time synchronization between devices. When sharing:

  • Anonymous authentication is used — no personal data (name, email, phone number) is collected
  • Shared data includes: shopping list items, quantities, shopping session history
  • Data is accessible only to devices that joined the shared list
  • You can stop sharing at any time, which removes your data from the shared storage
  • When the last member leaves a shared list, all data is automatically deleted from Firebase

If you do not use the sharing feature, no data leaves your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Use advertising or ad networks
  • Use cookies or similar technologies
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other device data

Export and Import

The App allows you to export and import shopping lists as files. These files are created and stored on your device. The developer has no access to these files.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
