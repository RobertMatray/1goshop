1GoShop - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Overview

1GoShop ("the App") is a shopping list application developed by Robert Matray. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

1GoShop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All shopping list data is stored locally on your device only. The App does not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share data with third parties
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Use cookies or similar technologies
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other device data

Local Data Storage

The App stores your shopping lists and preferences (such as language and theme settings) locally on your device using standard device storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any third party
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App
  • Can be exported by you as a file for personal backup

Export and Import

The App allows you to export and import shopping lists as files. These files are created and stored on your device. The developer has no access to these files.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
