1GoShop

Simple and fast shopping list app.

Features

  • Multiple shopping lists for different stores
  • Real-time list sharing with family and friends
  • Active shopping mode with history tracking
  • Gesture controls (swipe, tap, drag & drop)
  • Data backup and restore
  • Import from clipboard (Apple Notes, Google Sheets...)
  • Dark mode and custom accent colors
  • Offline conflict resolution for shared lists
  • Available in 12 languages (SK, EN, DE, HU, UK, CS, ZH, ES, FR, IT, PL, PT)
